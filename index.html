Questions & Answers

Everything you were going to email us anyway — answered before you hit send.

Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?

No. Start the trial with nothing but an email address and mild optimism. We’ll only ask for a card when you decide to keep pretending you have this under control.

Can my whole team use the same inbox?

Yes. Everyone shares one inbox, sees the same conversations, and collision detection makes sure nobody replies twice — or, more commonly, that everybody assumes someone else already did.

Is the AI agent actually a bunch of people in India?

The Inbox Agent is powered by a proprietary blend of machine learning and a massive network of low wage workers stationed around the globe. We prefer the term “hybrid intelligence.”

Does Oatmeal replace my email client?

For customer conversations, yes — everything lives in the shared inbox. Your personal inbox full of unread newsletters remains entirely your own problem.
